FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does CV writing take? +
Standard CV writing takes 24–48 hours. Express delivery (12 hours) is available for an additional fee. Executive packages may take up to 72 hours to ensure the highest quality.
Can I upload my old CV for a revamp? +
Absolutely! You can upload your existing CV as a starting point. Our team will revamp, modernize, and optimize it while preserving your key achievements and experience.
Are your CVs ATS-friendly? +
Yes! Every CV we write is optimized for Applicant Tracking Systems. We use relevant keywords, proper formatting, and clean structure that ATS software can easily parse.
How long does a branding project take? +
Basic logo design: 3–5 business days. Full brand identity packages: 1–2 weeks. Complex rebranding projects: 2–4 weeks depending on scope and revisions required.
Do you offer revisions? +
All packages include free revisions. Standard: 2 revisions. Premium: 3 revisions. Executive: unlimited revisions within 30 days. We want you to be 100% satisfied.
What file formats do you deliver? +
CVs are delivered in PDF, DOCX, and editable formats. Design files come in PNG, SVG, PDF, and source files (AI/PSD) depending on the package selected.
How do I pay? +
We accept M-Pesa, bank transfer, PayPal, and major credit cards. You can also request a free consultation before committing to payment.
